The MENU button is labeled G / HOME / BACK on the device

Confirm action - press A
Cancel - press B

Open menu - press MENU

Toggle crop - hold MENU and press L2
Toggle gutter - hold MENU and press R2
Toggle fast forward - press L3

Sleep/wake - press and release POWER
Shutdown - press and hold POWER for 1 second

Relaunch drastic - open menu and press START
Kill drastic - hold MENU and press POWER

While charging:

Wake display - press any button (except POWER)
Launch drastic - press POWER
